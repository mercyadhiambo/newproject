Environmental Scanning

As part of the planning sessions, an environmental scan of MONAMI was conducted by both staff and independent consultant. This consisted of a SWOT(provided a snap shot of both the internal and external environment) analysis to assess MONAMI's internal environment, and PESTLE analysis(this raised key points on the external environment) to assess its task and micro-envinronments.Staff also coducted evaluation of their program achievement against the strategic objectives(2019-2023) for each thematic area.

MONAMI Strategic Focus 2019-2023

This strategic plan is visinary and flexible document, allowing it to convey both the necessary/possible future state of the organization as a whole, as well as allowing it to accommodate change and guide decision making operational, tactical and individual levels. In essence, it identifies strategic area to be addresed over the comming 5 years. Thus, new plan aims to consolidate and build upon MONAMI's existing strengths also provide framework for ongoing organizational policy advancements.

Delivering the Strategic Plan

For MONAMI, careful thought has been brought to bear on the delivery of the programs dictated by this plan. The capacity of the organization to efficiently and effectiveely realize its vision and mission has been built into the strategic planning. For this reason, the the organizational structure, and board and senoir management roles have been recvisted. Identifying a number of key functions and desirable practices and incorporating them into the implementation strategy.

About us

We love to begin.

  • Background Story

    MONAMI Foundation is a non-profit making foundation, established and registered in kenya.The foundation's name ,MONAMI, is derived from the french word 'Mon Amie' which means 'My Friend' in English.The organization is founded on the tenet of 'friendship and service to the vulnerable in the society'. MONAMI foundation envisions a continent with improved livelihoods in which all can lead a self-determined life in dignity and social justice, free from hunger, diseases and poverty through empowering communities in Africa to sustainably improve their quality of life.

  • Our Vision

    A continent in which al can lead a self-determined life in dignity and social justice, free from hunger, disease and poverty.

  • Our Mission

    To create self-reliant communities in Africa that sustainably impprove their quality of lives.

SENIOR MANAGEMENT

Senior management team gives effect to the board by developing strategicoptions for board's consideration and approval,preparing annual organizational work plan,delivering objectives set in the work plan and also constructing effective relationships with partner organizations and maintaining good communication with the public.

RESOURCE MOBILIZATION

This wonderful team leads the MONAMI's program on coordinating and advising on fundraising activities and the management of restricted/donor and unrestricted funds and contracts and lead efforts to optimise resources and income generation to support achievement of her mission.

The BOARD

MONAMI is Non-Governmental organization and is managed by two executive organs-the Executive Board and the Secretariat.
The Board makes executive decisions regarding the operation of the organization and provides visionary leadership for sustainability of organization as a champion for a free, just and equitable society.
